About Lionel Messi

Lionel Andrés Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays for and captains La Liga club Barcelona and the Argentina national team. Born and raised in central Argentina, Messi relocated to Spain to join Barcelona at age 13, for whom he made his competitive debut aged 17 in October 2004. He established himself as an integral player for the club within the next three years, and in his first uninterrupted season in 2008–09 he helped Barcelona achieve the first treble in Spanish football.
